Farm-as-Shares Investment Model

One of the biggest innovations in AgriTrade is the fractional farmland investment model.

Example from your concept:

A farm requires ₹40,000 for cultivation.
The amount is divided into small shares.
Investors can buy shares with low entry cost.
Profits are distributed based on ownership percentage.

This makes agriculture accessible even to middle-class investors.

Your MSME document also explains this as:

“Fractional ownership of farmland through digital shares.”
